Wolf

The earliest drawings of wolves are in caves in southern Europe and date from 20,000 B.C

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus
  • Average Weight: 66-180 lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 5-6 years
  • Habitat: Woodlands, forests, grasslands and deserts

The wolf is a hunter. Living about 6 years and in different types of areas around the world.
